How This ATS Resume Checker Works

Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) are used by most companies to filter resumes before a human recruiter ever sees them. This ATS Resume Checker compares your resume content with the job description to identify keyword matches, missing skills, and formatting issues that could lower your ATS score.

Example: Resume vs Job Description

If a job description mentions skills like JavaScript, HTML, CSS, GitHub and your resume lacks these keywords, ATS may reject your application automatically. Our tool highlights exactly what is missing so you can optimize your resume.

Why ATS Optimization Matters

Over 75% of resumes are rejected by ATS before reaching recruiters. Optimizing your resume with relevant keywords, clean formatting, and clear job alignment dramatically increases your chances of getting shortlisted.

Common Resume Mistakes (ATS)

  • Using images, tables, or graphics
  • Missing job-specific keywords
  • Complex formatting
  • Generic resumes for all jobs
